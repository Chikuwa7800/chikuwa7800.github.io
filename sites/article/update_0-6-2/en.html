Alpha 0.6.2 Update Was Released!

Additons

Added hostiles, Warrior Husk, Knife-wielding Stray, Glaivemaster Bogged, Wizard Bogged.

Added items, Warrior Helm, Bash Shield.

Changes

The Wizard Drowned enemy has been removed from the spawn pool.

Renamed the Spearman's Hat to Assault Hat.

Zombies and Husks now have a 15% chance of spawning as a Zombie/Husk Leader carrying a flag.

Zombies and Husks now have a 19% chance of spawning as a Farmer Zombie/arrior Husk.

On Hard difficulty, Farmer Zombies now have a 25% chance to spawn additional zombies when damaged; this mechanic also applies to Warrior Husks.

The damage dealt by the Bone Crusher and Spearman Skeleton has been reduced.

Skeleton Horse Trap now has a 1% chance to spawn, replacing Skeletons.

Skeleton Horse trap now despawns 1 minute after spawning.

Bells no longer ring when struck by magic.

Fixes

Fixed an issue preventing the Wakame Sword from being crafted using a Magic Crafting Table.
