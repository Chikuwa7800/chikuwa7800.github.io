Changes

  • Mana capacity and regeneration now scale with the player's highest reached XP level.
  • Reduced the max mana bonus from Magic Core [Add Mana capacity] (50 → 10).
  • Slightly enhanced the visual effects for Thunder Screen, Atter Raggio, Thanatholos.
  • Flame Blast magic level increased to level 3.
  • The death message when a player dies from the Wet Status Effect has been changed from "drowned and died" to "submerged".
  • Flint Lance in camp tent chests have been replaced with Flint Knife.
  • Removed the recipe for smelting raw ore blocks into blocks.
  • Players can no longer cast other spells while chanting.

The visual appearance of various spells has been changed!

  • VFX improved.
  • The type of magic used is easier to identify from a distance.
  • Some magics were changed damage and area of effect.

Technical Changes

  • Renewed the fireballs fired by Flame Blast.
  • Changed the visual effects on used Magic Cores.
  • Reduced the flight time of Bolt Strike and Zephyr Blade.
  • Toned down Level 1 magic effects for better visibility.
  • Updated the Zephyr Blade's terrain hit effects and sounds.
  • Reduced the volume of Bolt Strike's hit sound.
  • Some terrain hit effects and sounds of all level 1 magics will now only play when hitting an entity.
  • The Japanese spelling of "Mana" was changed from English to Japanease.
  • Changed the base item for all non-right-click items from Wooden Pickaxes to Diamond Pickaxes.

    • Note: These items can no longer be used as fuel in furnaces!

  • Lances created in v0.6.3 or earlier have been renamed to "Old Spear".
  • Water Magic damage sound changed from "drowning" to "hurt".
  • "ls_trip_and_magic" folder name changed to "ch_trip_and_magic".
  • Level 1 Magic Projectiles will now despawn if the chunk they are about to move into in the next tick is not loaded.
  • Level 1 Magic projectiles no longer trigger special behaviors when touching specific blocks. Zephyr Blade is an exception.
  • Level 1 Magic projectiles are no longer summoned with an Area Effect Cloud passenger.
  • Changed the impact sound for Frost Bullet.
  • Zephyr Blades no longer deal strong knockback to entities.
  • Reduced the duration of the abyss effect applied by void magics.
  • Reduced the damage dealt by the abyss effect.
  • Revised the English phrasing to make it more appropriate.

  • Fixed Bugs in This Update

  • Atter Raggio was incorrectly named Atterraggio.
  • Mana regeneration particles were not appearing correctly.
  • Copper Lance required Netherite Ingot for repairs.
  • The Japanese translation where Magic Core's mana bonus was incorrectly labeled as "MP" instead of "マナ". Which means it should have been displayed as "Mana," but it was displayed as "MP"!
  • Flint weapons are incorrectly displaying "burning" as an added skill.
  • The death message caused by the "wet" status effect includes a period at the end.
  • The death message for status effects inflicted by other mobs is not defined.
  • Wave Surfing's damage type is "magic".
  • Players cannot place a core item into Magic Crafting Table.
  • Even if players place an material into Magic Crafting Table, the material is not consumed.
  • If Ginunga enters a Flinched or Stunned state during the tick in which it initiates an attack, it will subsequently become immobile.

  • Other minor adjustments...!
