Ch's Trip and Magic 0.7.0 Update Release Note

New Features

  • Added level 3 Spellbooks.
  • Added Magic Level Up Core Lv.3.
  • Added level 3 magics.
  • Added Lighting Rapier.
  • Added Takhelan Wand
  • Added Craive
  • Added Teleportation Scroll
  • Added Skywalk Enchantment

    • New Magics are Available Now!

    List of the added magics

    • Keraut Llone
    • Pyro Wave
    • Glacies Wave
    • Gust Burst
    • Void Veil
    • Water Vale

    Changes

    • Mana capacity and regeneration now scale with the player's highest reached XP level.
    • Reduced the max mana bonus from Magic Core [Add Mana capacity] (50 → 10).
    • Slightly enhanced the visual effects for Thunder Screen.
    • Renewed the fireballs fired by Flame Blast.

    Thunder Screen

    • VFX improved.
    • The type of magic used is easier to identify from a distance.
    • Even outside the magic's area of effect, it provides a new visual experience for other players.
    • Changed damage and area of effect.

    Technical Changes

  • Reduced the flight time of Bolt Strike and Zephyr Blade.
  • Toned down Level 1 magic effects for better visibility.
  • Updated the Zephyr Blade's terrain hit effects and sounds.
  • Reduced the volume of Bolt Strike's hit sound.
  • Some terrain hit effects and sounds of all level 1 magics will now only play when hitting an entity.
  • The Japanese spelling of "Mana" was changed from English to Japanease.
  • Changed the base item for all non-right-click items from Wooden Pickaxes to Diamond Pickaxes.

    • Note: These items can no longer be used as fuel in furnaces!

  • Lances created in v0.6.3 or earlier have been renamed to "Old Spear".

  • Fixed Bugs in This Update

  • Atter Raggio was incorrectly named Atterraggio.
  • Mobs failed to be lanched when killed by the Atter Raggio.
  • Mana regeneration particles were not appearing correctly.
  • Copper Lance required Netherite Ingot for repairs.
  • The Japanese translation where Magic Core's mana bonus was incorrectly labeled as "MP" instead of "マナ". Which means it should have been displayed as "Mana," but it was displayed as "MP"!

  • Other minor adjustments...!
